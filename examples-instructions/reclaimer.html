GAME-BUILDER does a lot of object pooling, as such you need something to claim objects back for re-use. The reclaimer module does just that.

Controls

Pressing A Adds the three objects used in the demo back to the screen
Pressing S Claims all the active objects
Pressing Z Claims the green square only
Pressing C Claims everything but the green sqaure
Pressing P Destroys all objects. After doing this the demo needs to be refreshed
