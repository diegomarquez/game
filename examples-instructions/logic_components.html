This demo shows how components can modify the behaviour of objects in a decoupled way. In the demo, each of the squares has the same component type attached to it, but with different arguments. That is why they are progressibly more twitchy from left to right.

The component shown in the demo is not particularly useful, but hey, it's just a demo.

There is no interaction in this demo, As soon as you are bored of staring at it, you can proceed.
